# Pages and Core Functions

## Pages

- Home: Product categories, featured parts, quick search.
- Search / Browse: Query, sort, filters, category navigation.
- Product Details: Images, specs, fitment, price, add-to-cart, seller info.
- Parts Finder / Fitment: VIN or vehicle selector, compatibility results.
- Cart: Line items, quantity, price breakdown, shipping selection.
- Checkout: Payment, shipping address, promo codes, order review.
- Orders: Order history, tracking, receipts, returns.
- Account: Profile, addresses, payment methods, settings.
- Wishlist / Favorites: Saved parts and alerts for restock/price.
- Reviews & Q&A: Submit/read reviews, ask seller questions.
- Help & Support: FAQs, contact, returns policy.
- Admin / Seller Dashboard: Inventory, orders, pricing, product management.
- Legal: Privacy, terms.

## Core Functions

- Search: Full-text, suggestions, voice/barcode search.
- Filters & Sorting: Brand, price, condition, shipping, rating.
- Vehicle Fitment Check: VIN lookup, year/make/model matching.
- Add to Cart & Cart Management: Quantity, bundles, saved carts.
- Checkout & Payments: Integrations (Stripe/PayPal), receipts.
- Order Tracking: Shipment status, ETA, tracking links.
- Auth & Profiles: Email/Social login, MFA, account recovery.
- Wishlist / Alerts: Price drops, back-in-stock notifications.
- Reviews & Ratings: CRUD reviews, moderation tools.
- Image Zoom / Gallery: High-res, pinch-to-zoom, lightbox.
- Barcode / VIN Scanner: Quick part lookup via camera.
- Inventory & Availability: Real-time stock, multiple warehouses.
- Shipping Options & Calculations: Rates, carriers, estimated delivery.
- Promotions & Coupons: Apply codes, validation, discounts.
- Recommendations: Related parts, upsells, AI suggestions.
- Notifications: Push, in-app, email for orders/alerts.
- Admin Tools: CRUD products, adjust pricing, manage orders, reports.
- Analytics & Logging: Usage metrics, sales dashboards, error logging.
